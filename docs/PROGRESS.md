### **🔐 Role-Based Access Control**

#### **User Roles & Permissions:**
- **Owner**: Full access to everything, can manage billing, invite admins
- **Admin**: Can manage team, settings, all business operations
- **Manager**: Can create/manage invoices, expenses, view reports
- **Employee**: Can view assigned items, create expenses, limited access

#### **UI Role Indicators:**
- **Owner Badge**: 👑 Gold crown with "Owner"
- **Admin Badge**: 🛡️ Silver shield with "Admin"
- **Manager Badge**: 📋 Blue clipboard with "Manager"
- **Employee Badge**: 👤 Gray user icon with "Employee"
